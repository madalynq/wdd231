Hooked

Site Plan

Site Name: Hooked

Hooks are the main tool used to crochet, and I liked the idea of word play relating to the idea that crocheting is an activity/ hobby/ pasttime that many people get "hooked" on.

Site Purpose:

To provide a community where people can come together, teach and learn from each other, and do projects together as well.

Scenarios:

  • What do I need to start learning how to crochet?
  • What are some good beginner projects that I can do?
  • Some patterns show symbols, how do I know what they stand for?
  • Where can I go to get feedback on a project that I am currently working on?
  • Where can I share my work and view others, and get new ideas for projects that I might want to start?
